Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with EqualEd? +

Getting started with EqualEd is easy! Simply create an account, complete your learning profile, and our AI will recommend personalized courses and resources based on your needs and preferences.

What accessibility features are available? +

EqualEd offers various accessibility features including high contrast mode, text-to-speech, adjustable font sizes, keyboard navigation, and screen reader compatibility. Visit our Accessibility page for more details.

How does the adaptive learning system work? +

Our adaptive learning system uses AI to analyze your learning patterns, strengths, and areas for improvement. It then adjusts the content, pace, and teaching methods to match your unique learning style.

Can I track my progress? +

Yes! You can track your progress through detailed analytics, achievement badges, and progress reports. These tools help you monitor your learning journey and celebrate your accomplishments.
