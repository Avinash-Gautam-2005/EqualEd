Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with EqualEd?

You can create an account by clicking the "Get Started" button on our homepage. We'll guide you through the process of setting up your profile and selecting courses.

What accessibility features do you offer?

We offer a range of accessibility features including screen reader compatibility, high contrast mode, text-to-speech, and customizable font sizes.

How do I request technical support?

You can reach our technical support team through the contact form above, or by emailing support@equaled.org. We aim to respond to all support requests within 24 hours.

Can I try EqualEd before committing?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial that gives you full access to our platform and courses. No credit card required.
